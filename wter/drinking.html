Tonimas Table Water

Introduction

Established in 2008, Tonimas Water is a widely acknowledged business organization engaged in manufacturing and supplying a wide spectrum of Packaged Tonimas bottled Water to our esteemed patrons.

Our offered product range is processed in a well segregated and hygienic premises, to ensure a safe and consumable water. Products we offer are widely accepted and appreciated by the large number of clients and users due to their optimum quality.

Tonimas Water also offers a customised line of our drinking water products for the big occassion i.e. your weddings, AGMs, etc. In addition to that, we also offer delivery services as requested by the client.

Product Line

Our product range at Tonimas Water include: –

  • 50cl bottled water
  • 75cl bottled water
  • 150cl bottled water
  • 18.5 litres Water Dispenser refill
